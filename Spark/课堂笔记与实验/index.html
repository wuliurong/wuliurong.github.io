df代码：df3=df.groupBy(“publish”).count().show()

4、将评分换算为百分制并新增一列（列名随意）

df代码：df4=df.select(“*”).withColumn(“百分制评分”,df[“score”]*10).show()

sql代码：df4=spark.sql(‘select id,name,price,publish,url,score,score*10 AS newsore from book’).show()

5、把书目按照评分从高到低进行排列

df代码：df5=df.select(“*”).orderBy(df[“score”]).show()

sql代码：df5=spark.sql(“SELECT * FROM book ORDER BY score “).show()
